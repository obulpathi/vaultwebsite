What is Time Vault Coin?

It is a cryptocurrency built with novel time travel technology. If someone steals your coins, with Time Vault Coin, you can go back in time and restore your coins.

How does it work?

With TVC you can crate two different kinds of accounts: Standard Accounts and Vault Accounts. Standard accounts behave much like Bitcoin accounts and allow you to send and receive money for daily purposes. Vault accounts, built with time-travel technology, make your coins hack-proof. A Vault is backed by two key pairs, one online and one offline. If someone, hacks your computer and steals your coins, the transactions have to wait in blockchain for a configurable period of timeout to get confirmed. Using your offline keypair, you can revert the transactions not initiated by you and restore your coins to a different account.
